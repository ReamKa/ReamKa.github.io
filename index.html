Taxieur

Only one type of journey is reimbursed by the Social Security for people with disabilities: the round trip from their home to their care center. With Taxieur, we are fighting to get students reimbursed for their 3 way trip : from their home to their school and ending at their care center. This project got us the 2nd place in our University.

Coded in PHP, HTML/CSS, and MySQL.
